1. Introduction

NOVAGAPP is committed to protecting user privacy through transparent and responsible data practices. This policy applies to information collected through mobile and web interfaces, customer support channels, and related communication features.

NOVAGAPP acts as the controller for personal data processed for account management, platform operations, analytics, safety, and service delivery. Users are encouraged to review this policy regularly to remain informed about current practices.

Depending on platform use, NOVAGAPP may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: name, email address, username, profile image, and account identifiers.
  • User Content: posts, reels, comments, messages, product listings, uploaded media, and related metadata.
  • Transaction and Activity Data: order details, cart actions, item interactions, settings preferences, and engagement records.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device model, app version, language preferences, and access timestamps.

NOVAGAPP uses collected information for legitimate business and operational purposes, including:

  • Creating, maintaining, and securing user accounts.
  • Providing platform features such as social interactions, commerce tools, personalization, and notifications.
  • Processing and managing orders, account preferences, and service requests.
  • Monitoring performance, detecting abuse, preventing fraud, and enforcing platform rules.
  • Improving user experience through analytics, testing, and feature development.
  • Responding to customer support requests and legal or regulatory obligations.
  • Delivering and measuring promotional and advertising experiences.
