Privacy Center

Your privacy matters

We are committed to protecting your personal information and giving you full control over how your data is used on Fluence.

What data we collect

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Profile details you choose to share
  • Messages, comments, and interactions
  • Usage data to improve app experience

How your data is used

  • To provide core app features
  • To keep your account secure
  • To personalize content and settings
  • To improve performance and reliability

Your controls

You can control who can see your content, message you, or comment on your posts from the Settings pages.

Go to Settings → Privacy, Messages, or Comments to update your preferences.

Data security

Your data is protected using secure infrastructure, encrypted storage, and strict access controls.

Need more help?

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, you can contact our support team anytime from the Help & Support section.
